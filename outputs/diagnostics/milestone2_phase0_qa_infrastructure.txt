Note on completeness gate
--------------------------
  The 60% completeness threshold is calibrated for multi-tile epoch mosaics.
  Single tiles (like the canary) have lower completeness due to primary beam
  attenuation near edges. The canary script uses separate, looser acceptance
  criteria (n_recovered >= 3) rather than the full three-gate epoch QA.

  Wide epoch mosaics (Jan 25 22h, ~15° RA) also show low completeness (24%)
  because many NVSS sources >= 50 mJy fall in low-sensitivity edge regions.
  This threshold may need calibration based on production epoch runs.
